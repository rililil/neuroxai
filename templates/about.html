About This Project

Advancing neurological diagnostics through Handwriting Analysis and Explainable AI.

This system is a research prototype developed by students from the College of Computer Science and Engineering. Our mission is to bridge machine learning precision with clinical interpretability.

Project Objectives

  • Biomarker Analysis: Identifying neurological markers in handwriting data.
  • ML Training: Developing robust detection models for early-stage screening.
  • Explainability: Using SHAP to provide transparent diagnostic evidence.
  • Clinical Utility: Offering doctors a streamlined tool for decision support.
